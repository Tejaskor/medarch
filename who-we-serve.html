Medarch Inc. is a healthcare IT company that aims to empower healthcare providers with robust healthcare software solutions that can transform their practice to a completely digital landscape. Our long-term goal is to create and equip healthcare practices with a well-connected healthcare IT ecosystem

eCarehealth is one of Medarch Inc.'s products, which is basically electronic medical records software that helps healthcare practices secure the storage and management of patients' protected health information. Its additional features help practices from streamlining data management to improving patient engagement.

eCareMD is a type of care coordination software that helps healthcare providers deliver coordinated care virtually while aligning themselves with the programs of the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services such as CCM, RPM, TCM, AWV, etc.

IntraConnects is another product of Medarch Inc. and has proven to be essential for healthcare practices to build a well-connected healthcare ecosystem. It offers robust EHR integration and interoperability options for healthcare providers using multiple software systems.

eCareScribe is an AI-powered healthcare software developed by Medarch. It mainly uses advanced technology like AI and ML for natural language processes and redefining documentation in healthcare practices. The software transcribes conversations between providers and patients in real-time and updates them in EHR with enhanced accuracy

Medarch Billing is a medical billing software that simplifies billing for healthcare practices by streamlining their billing processes from prior authorization to claim submission, ICD 10 coding, auditing, and denial management. It helps healthcare practices to streamline and improve their revenue cycle.

Just taking your digital practice is not enough. With Medarch Marketing, you improve your practice's online presence and position the practices online to truly allow them to deliver virtual care.
